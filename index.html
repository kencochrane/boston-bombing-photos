Boston Bombing Photo Upload

Help the FBI catch the people responsible for the deadly Boston Marathon Bombings.
Upload your photos and videos here, and we will send them to the FBI to help with their investigation

Why?

The FBI is looking for any photos and videos that people have of the Boston Marathon Bombing to help find the people responsible. Every photo/video helps when piecing the puzzle together.

They are currently asking people to email them their stuff to boston@ic.fbi.gov. But as people are aware emailing large photos and movies isn't always possible. So we setup this site to make it easier to collect the photos and videos.

How does it work?

All photos are uploaded to an Amazon S3 bucket. We will then contact the FBI and either give them access to the same bucket, or send them the whole collection, what ever they want us to do.

Spread the word

Help us spread the word, tell your friends and family, and anyone else who might have been at the marathon to either email their photos/videos or use a site like this one to upload them.
